Question: The table shows the number of pencils each student has.
Student | Pencils
Alice   | 12
Ben     | 9
Cindy   | 15
How many pencils do Alice and Cindy have together?
Let's think step by step.
Alice has 12 pencils.
Cindy has 15 pencils.
We add them: 12 + 15 = 27.
The answer is 27.
